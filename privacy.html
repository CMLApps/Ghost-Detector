Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Cloud Motion Lab LLC ("we," "our," or "us") built Ghost Detector EMF Scanner ("the App") as a paranormal entertainment application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. The App does not require an account, login, or registration. Your scan history, session logs, and preferences are stored only on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

Sensors and Camera

The App uses your device's magnetometer to read ambient electromagnetic field strength for the EMF scanning feature. The App requests access to your camera for the Night Vision detection mode. The App requests access to your microphone for the EVP scanning feature, which captures ambient audio to drive an on-screen visualization. All sensor readings, camera frames, and microphone input are processed entirely on your device. No sensor data, video frames, audio, or recordings are ever uploaded or stored persistently. Microphone input is analyzed in memory and discarded.

Photo Library

The App may request permission to save detection videos or session screenshots to your photo library. The App does not read existing photos and does not upload anything to any server.

Analytics

The App uses TelemetryDeck, a privacy-focused analytics service, to collect anonymous usage signals such as which screens are viewed and which features are used. These signals contain no personal information, no device identifiers, and cannot be used to identify individual users. For more information, see TelemetryDeck's Privacy Policy.

Advertising

The free version of the App displays ads served by Unity Ads (Unity Technologies). Unity Ads may collect limited device information for ad delivery and frequency capping. With your permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt, Unity Ads may use the IDFA (advertising identifier) to deliver more relevant ads. You can decline tracking and still use every feature of the App. For more information, see Unity's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

The App offers in-app purchases, including a weekly subscription and a lifetime access option, processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID and purchase receipts from Apple but does not receive any personal information. We do not collect or have access to your payment information. All purchase management, including refunds and cancellations, is handled through your Apple ID account settings.

Data Storage

All app data, including session history, scan logs, preferences, and purchase status, is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 12 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

TelemetryDeck — for anonymous, privacy-focused usage analytics
RevenueCat — for subscription management
Unity Ads — for advertising in the free version
Apple StoreKit — for processing in-app purchases

Entertainment Disclaimer

The App is intended for entertainment purposes only. Detections, readings, and "ghost" identifications are generated for atmospheric and recreational use. The App does not actually detect supernatural entities and is not a scientific instrument.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
